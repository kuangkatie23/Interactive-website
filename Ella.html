Ella

Your name is Ella, you work as a shoe creator of a famous brand known worldwide.
You live with your 2 stepsisters in a mansion in California

It’s 7am in the morning and your phone rings,

“Hello Ella speaking-”
“Ella, we need your ideas on a shoe that would match a new outfit we got. ASAP.
I’m sending you a picture now.”

15 minutes later,
Ella walks downstairs drawn by the scent of her sister making breakfast.
“Good morning, what are you cooking?” Ella asked her sister “Eggs with pancakes”. came her reply

Ella eats breakfast quickly, grabs her bag and heads out.
She meets her friend, Eleanor, outside and together they head to work.
On their way they talked about all the new popular movies that came out and laughed happily enjoying their lives.

When at work they go their separate ways.
Ella plops down in her seat, and starts reviewing the guest list.
She finds out many famous people are there. The first name that catches her eye is James.
Oh my god, she thought to herself. Her dad is a huge fan of James.
She wonders if they will meet, if he even shows up to her fashion show.
She has not seen him in months and kinda missed him thinking about him now.

As the day comes to an end you head home. A few minutes later you realize you left your phone behind.
Your’e wondering if you should just leave it there and come back for it tomorrow,
but you realize you might get lots of important phone calls during this period of time with the fashion show and everything, so you head back in.

As you walk in, you accidentally bump into someone.
You say sorry and realize he looks awfully familiar.
Should you

A. Ask "Do I know You?"

B. Brush off the thought and go retrive your phone
